    How risk is calculated

    Risk is based on simple rule thresholds using environmental indicators (e.g. rainfall and temperature). The goal is transparency and discussion, not prediction.

    Data & limitations

    • Environmental data may be incomplete or delayed.
    • Risk indicates favourable conditions, not confirmed parasite presence.
    • This tool does not replace veterinary advice.
